About

This annual event promotes a peer support and mentoring group that helps to develop junior faculty without tenure in Northern America whose research area spans visualization. This experience provides a unique mentoring opportunity that complements the available resources to faculty who are starting to navigate the world of academia and aids their transition from graduate school or postdoctoral positions to the next stages of their career.
