Schedule

This year, we are going to use a slightly different structure for the sessions. Each "session" below is a 90-minute slot split in the following way:

  • - 45 minutes of presentation ("listen")
  • - 30 minutes of discussion ("talk"), split in two blocks of breakout groups and discussion rejoinder
  • - 15 minutes of unstructured talking, snacking, stretching, resting

In addition, you will decide the content for Wednesday. Bring ideas!

*** Disclaimer: The schedule is subject to change
